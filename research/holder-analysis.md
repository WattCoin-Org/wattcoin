## 4. Organic Growth Indicators

### 4.1 Pattern Recognition

**Organic Growth Patterns:**

1. **Steady Accumulation**
   - Daily holder growth: 2-5%
   - No sudden spikes >20% in 24h
   - Correlated with positive news/development milestones

2. **Healthy Distribution**
   - Top 10 holders < 35%
   - Median holder balance < 0.1% of supply
   - Gini coefficient < 0.7

3. **Genuine Trading**
   - Volume/liquidity ratio < 5x
   - Bid-ask spread < 2%
   - Consistent trading across multiple DEXes

**Artificial/Manipulation Patterns:**

1. **Pump Signals**
   - Sudden holder spikes >50% in 24h
   - Top 10 concentration >50%
   - Volume/liquidity ratio >8x

2. **Wash Trading Indicators**
   - Same wallets on both sides of trades
   - Circular trading patterns
   - Volume without price movement

3. **Bot Activity**
   - High frequency, low value trades
   - Transactions at regular intervals
   - Multiple wallets from same funding source
